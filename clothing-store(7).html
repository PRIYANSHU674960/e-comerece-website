Refund & Returns Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2026 · Last Updated: 1 March 2026

1. Returns Window

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date. Items must be in their original, unworn condition with all tags attached and in original packaging. Sale items marked "Final Sale" are not eligible for returns.

2. How to Initiate a Return
  • Email returns@voge.in or WhatsApp us at +91 98765 43210 with your order number and reason for return
  • Our team will respond within 24 hours with a Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA) number
  • Ship the item(s) back to our warehouse address using any traceable courier
  • Return shipping costs are borne by the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect
3. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return (2–3 business days), we will notify you of approval or rejection. Approved refunds are processed to your original UPI payment method within 5–7 business days. Bank processing times may vary.

4. Exchanges

We offer free size exchanges on all full-price items. WhatsApp us with your order number and the size you need. Subject to stock availability.

5. Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photographs. We will arrange a free pickup and send a replacement or full refund at our expense.

6. Non-Returnable Items
  • Innerwear, swimwear, and pierced jewellery (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items marked "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Items that show signs of wear, damage, or alteration

Reseller & Dropship Guide

Option 1 — Become a Flipkart Seller
1
Register on Flipkart Seller Hub
Visit seller.flipkart.com and click "Start Selling". You need a GSTIN, bank account, and a valid email to register.
2
List your own products
Upload product photos, descriptions, pricing and stock. Flipkart provides a seller dashboard to manage orders and payments. You can list clothing, accessories and more.
3
Use Flipkart's fulfilment (optional)
Enroll in Flipkart Smart Fulfilment — send stock to their warehouse and they handle storage, packing and shipping for you.
4
Payments every 7–14 days
Flipkart settles payments directly to your bank after deducting their marketplace commission (typically 5–20% depending on category).
Option 2 — Meesho Reseller (No investment needed)
1
Download Meesho app
Available on Android and iOS. Register with your phone number and basic details. No GSTIN required to start as a reseller.
2
Share products and earn margin
Browse Meesho's catalogue of thousands of ethnic wear, western wear and accessories. Share product links via WhatsApp. Add your own profit margin on top of the supplier price.
3
Meesho handles everything else
No need to hold stock. Meesho directly ships to your customer. You earn the margin difference. Payouts weekly via UPI.
